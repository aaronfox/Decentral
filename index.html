What is DeCentral?

DeCentral is a decentralized lending service that connects micro loans with short term investors. With DeCentral, individuals who normally are ineligible for loans can now receive loans at an interest rate lower than a PayDay loan. Individuals who can’t receive a loan from typical banks, will pay $400% interest rates on a PayDay Loan! At DeCentral, we believe this is taking advantage of our own people, and we have cut that number in half! Through our very progressive and encouraging Loan Credit Rating System, lendee’s will be able to cut their interest rate from our starting 200% to 30% upon successfully completing 13 repaid loans. If you wish read more, or have questions; follow us over to the FAQ Page.
